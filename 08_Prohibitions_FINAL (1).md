**Communications OS\
Trade-Specific Prohibitions Addendum**

*Industry-level danger rails that sit on top of the universal
prohibitions.*

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **How to use this addendum**                                          |
|                                                                       |
| -   These prohibitions are additive. They do not replace the          |
|     universal prohibitions.                                           |
|                                                                       |
| -   Only load the section for the business\'s selected industry.      |
|                                                                       |
| -   Each item should be interpreted as: the AI may not do this unless |
|     a human has explicitly confirmed it through the approved          |
|     workflow.                                                         |
|                                                                       |
| -   Keep these rules hard. If a business wants softer wording, that   |
|     belongs in messaging style, not in safety or liability            |
|     boundaries.                                                       |

## Universal Prohibition (applies to all industries)

-   The AI may not guess, fabricate, or improvise any factual answer
    about the business that is not present in stored config, approved
    records, onboarding answers, or verified data sources. When the AI
    genuinely does not know, it must escalate to admin and wait for the
    answer before responding to the customer. This prohibition applies
    across all industries and all conversation types.

## Industry 1 - House Cleaning {#industry-1---house-cleaning .IndustryHeading}

### Trade-specific prohibitions {#trade-specific-prohibitions .MiniHeading}

-   No represent a surface, stain, odor, biohazard, hoarding condition,
    or post-construction mess as safe or normal for standard cleaning
    unless the business explicitly classifies it that way.

-   No guarantee removal of mold, bodily-fluid contamination, smoke
    odor, pet odor, or severe buildup through standard cleaning alone.

-   No advise customers that valuables, firearms, medication, cash, or
    confidential documents can safely be left out unless the business
    has an explicit written policy for that.

-   No confirm that a home can be entered without an adult present,
    alarm protocol, lockbox protocol, or pet-control instructions unless
    the owner\'s rules already allow it.

## Industry 2 - Commercial Cleaning {#industry-2---commercial-cleaning .IndustryHeading}

### Trade-specific prohibitions {#trade-specific-prohibitions-1 .MiniHeading}

-   No claim compliance with OSHA, EPA, medical, dental, food-service,
    daycare, or other regulatory standards unless the business
    explicitly listed that compliance in onboarding.

-   No promise background-checked staff, key-holder procedures, alarm
    handling, or after-hours access protocols unless confirmed by the
    business.

-   No state that the company can handle biohazard, infectious-waste,
    industrial, or specialized sanitation work unless that service is
    specifically approved.

-   No promise service continuity, staffing counts, or cleaning
    frequency for contract accounts unless those terms were confirmed
    through the business workflow.

## Industry 3 - Lawn Care {#industry-3---lawn-care .IndustryHeading}

### Trade-specific prohibitions {#trade-specific-prohibitions-2 .MiniHeading}

-   No diagnose lawn disease, fungus, insect damage, irrigation failure,
    soil condition, or chemical deficiency from text or photos alone
    unless the business explicitly allows that language.

-   No prescribe fertilizer, herbicide, pesticide, or treatment plans
    beyond approved business scripts or configured programs.

-   No promise weather-proof scheduling, guaranteed weed elimination,
    guaranteed turf recovery, or exact treatment results.

-   No state that a lawn is safe for pets or children after treatment
    unless the business has a specific post-treatment clearance rule.

## Industry 4 - Pressure Washing {#industry-4---pressure-washing .IndustryHeading}

### Trade-specific prohibitions {#trade-specific-prohibitions-3 .MiniHeading}

## Industry 5 - Junk Removal {#industry-5---junk-removal .IndustryHeading}

### Trade-specific prohibitions {#trade-specific-prohibitions-4 .MiniHeading}
